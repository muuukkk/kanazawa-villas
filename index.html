03Neighborhood

The city,
close enough
to walk.

Most of what makes Kanazawa worth visiting — the gardens, the teahouse districts, the market — is within walking distance, or one short bus ride away.

01

Kenroku-en Garden

15 min by bus

One of Japan's three great gardens. Try the early morning free-entry hour.

02

Higashi Chaya District

20 min on foot

Lattice-fronted teahouses. Most beautiful at dusk, when the lanterns come on.

03

Omicho Market

15 min on foot

Local vegetables, fish from the Sea of Japan. The villa kitchens ask to be cooked in.

04

21st Century Museum

18 min on foot

Contemporary art that meets you on a daily, unhurried scale.

05

Nishi Chaya District

6 min from Nomachi

The smallest of the three teahouse quarters. Quieter, less photographed.

06

Myōryū-ji (Ninja Temple)

4 min from Nomachi

A temple of hidden mechanisms. Booking essential.

07

Kanazawa Station

7 min from Horikawa

The Tsuzumi-mon gate sets the tone — your trip begins on arrival.

08

Kazuemachi Chaya

18 min on foot

A riverside teahouse district. The quietest of them all.

07FAQ

Questions,
answered.

A few things guests usually ask before they arrive. For anything else, send a message.

Q.01
When can we check in and out?
Check-in is from 16:00, check-out by 10:00. Earlier check-in or later check-out can sometimes be arranged — please ask.
Q.02
How does check-in work?
All villas use self check-in via keypad. After your booking is confirmed, we email the access code and detailed instructions.
Q.03
What languages can the host handle?
Japanese and English directly. Most other languages — Chinese, Korean, French, Spanish, German, etc. — via real-time AI translation through the messaging system.
Q.04
Why book direct, instead of Airbnb?
Direct bookings come without the OTA service fee — usually around 8% lower. Long-stay discounts (for 7+ nights or 28+ nights) are also only available through direct booking.
Q.05
Is there parking?
Nomachi and Shiragiku include one free on-site parking space. Horikawa uses a coin parking lot 3 minutes away (paid by the guest).
Q.06
Are the houses suitable for young children?
Yes. Baby cots and high chairs are available on request, free of charge. Please mention this when booking.
Q.07
What about long stays and remote work?
All villas have Wi-Fi suitable for video calls. Discounts apply at 7+ nights, with monthly rates available from 28 nights.
