06FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Q.01When can we check in and out?
Check-in is from 15:00, check-out by 11:00. Earlier check-in or later check-out can sometimes be arranged — please ask.
Q.02How does check-in work?
After booking, we send you a link to your private guest page. There you upload a passport photo (non-Japanese guests) and complete a short guest registry for everyone in your party — a step required by Japanese law. Once submitted, your check-in details (address, access, Wi-Fi, entrance keybox PIN) appear on the page. A staff member usually meets you at the villa for in-person ID verification. You can message us anytime through the guest page during your stay.
Q.03What's the difference between direct booking and Airbnb?
Direct booking (Nomachi) is the lowest rate — no OTA commission applies. Long-stay discounts (10% for 3+ nights, 15% for 4+ nights, 18% for 7+ nights) are direct-booking only. Shiragiku is currently bookable on Airbnb only.
Q.04Is there a minimum stay?
Yes — two nights minimum at all villas.
Q.05What languages can the host handle?
The host speaks Japanese only, but uses real-time translation tools to assist guests in any language. Replies typically arrive within the hour.
Q.06Is there parking?
Nomachi has free parking 2 minutes on foot from the villa. Shiragiku doesn't have private parking, but coin parking is available 1 minute away.
Q.07Can we stay with young children?
Yes. A high chair is available on request, free of charge — please mention this when booking.
Q.08What about long stays and remote work?
All villas have Wi-Fi suitable for video calls. Discounts apply from 3 nights onward, with the deepest direct-booking discount at 7+ nights.
