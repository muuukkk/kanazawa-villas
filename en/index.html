05FAQ

FAQ

Q.01Is there parking?
Nomachi: free parking 2 min on foot. Shiragiku: coin parking 1 min away.
Q.02Can we stay with young children?
Yes. A high chair is available on request, free of charge — please mention this when booking.
Q.03What about long stays and remote work?
All villas have Wi-Fi suitable for video calls. For discount details, see 02 Why book direct.
Q.04Is there a minimum stay?
Two nights minimum at both villas.
Q.05How does check-in work?
After booking, we send you a link to your private guest page. There you upload a passport photo (non-Japanese guests) and complete a short guest registry for everyone in your party — a step required by Japanese law. Once submitted, your check-in details (address, access, Wi-Fi, entrance keybox PIN) appear on the page. A staff member usually meets you at the villa for in-person ID verification. You can message us anytime through the guest page during your stay.
Q.06What languages can the host handle?
Japanese, plus real-time translation for virtually any language. Most replies within an hour.
Q.07What's the difference between direct booking and Airbnb?
Direct booking on Nomachi Villa is the lowest rate, with long-stay discounts. See 02 Why book direct for details. Shiragiku is currently bookable on Airbnb only.
